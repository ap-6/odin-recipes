Blueberry Cake

Image from www.rainbownourishments.com

Description

A simple recipe for blueberry cake.

Equipment

  • 8x8 pan

Ingredients

  • 2 tbs butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs separated
  • 1 + 1/2 cup flour
  • 1/3 cup milk
  • 1 + 1/2 cups blueberries (coated with flour)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350º
  2. Beat egg whites until peaks appear (stiff)
  3. Cream butter, sugar, egg yolks
  4. Gradually add flour and milk
  5. Fold in egg whites
  6. Pour 1/2 of mix into pan
  7. Add blueberries
  8. Top with remaining batter
  9. Bake for 35 minutes
